When writing within an article context, the first paragraph will always be larger, so as to indicate itself as an introduction.

The second paragraph (and every paragraph thereafter) will remain the base font size. This makes things a bit easier to read once you get past the main intro. There's no reason to make this any bigger, as we don't want the visual impact to disctract readers. Oh by the way, here's a link.
