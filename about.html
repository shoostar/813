I absolutely LOVE Hockey

It's inescapable; if you ever have a conversation with me, I can guarantee that at some point hockey will become part of the discussion. I've played it, refereed it, coached it, helped found a Tier 2 Junior A hockey team, and follow the Detroit Red Wings closer than most people regard their religion.

Gaming

While not near as often as I did as a youth, I still very much enjoy playing video games. I'm not a stuck-up snobby console fanboy; I'm proud to include myself amongst the Elite Race (PC). From the Battlefield to the depths of Diablo's fiery wastelands, I like and play 'em all.

Television

I don't spend a lot of time in front of the TV, although I've enjoyed watching Breaking Bad, Sons of Anarchy, Lost Ship, and Trailer Park Boys.

Food

Anything with meat and/or cheese. To expand on that a little bit, anything with meat and/or cheese.

Politics

I read enough of the daily news from around the world to know that I don't want to involve myself in politics. It's confusing, boring, and no matter what your opinion or stance is, you're probably wrong. What's the point?

Music

If it's too loud, you're too old! I'll listen to and can appreciate just about any music, but my heart and soul belong to the screaming stringed demons of Metal. I regret giving up playing bass guitar at a young age, but I've since picked up an electric guitar, and enjoy a strum or two from time to time.
